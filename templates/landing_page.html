Topic Search

Use this tool to find reviewers for a manuscript. Topic search will compile a list of potential reviewers given the abstract and title of an academic manuscript. The reviewers will be ranked by their likelihood of being in the field of research of the given manuscript.


Author Search

Use this tool to find reviewers for an authors body of work. Author Search will compile a list of an author's published papers. After allowing the user to select a subset, Author Search will return a list of potential reviewers ranked by their likelihood of being qualified enough to review thier work.
